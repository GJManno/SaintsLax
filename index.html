The Saints is a youth lacrosse club based out of Las Vegas, Nevada. Saints is for boys grades 2-12 who are interested in learning lacrosse in a fun and nuturing enviorment. No experience is necessary to participate. Full equipment will be required to play. Rental equipment is available on a first come, first serve basis.

If your child attends a private school west of the I-15 or if you child attends a Diocese school east of I-15, you are zoned to play for Saints per SNLA (Southern Nevada Lacrosse Asscociation). The SNLA is a wonderful resource for all things Nevada lacrosse. Need help purchasing gear?
